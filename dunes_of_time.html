Casey Vigor: The Dunes Of Time

The Dunes Of Time follows Casey Vigor as he ventures deep into the Kacia Desert in search of a rumored hidden temple. What begins as a simple exploration mission quickly spirals into a survival ordeal — and a discovery that threatens the entire galaxy.

1 — Into the Kacia Desert

Casey Vigor travels alone into the scorching Kacia Desert, guided only by fragmented coordinates and ancient Guardian records hinting at a lost temple buried beneath the dunes. His isotope core flickers under the heat, but he pushes forward, determined to uncover the truth.

Hours pass. His water runs low. His comms begin to fail. The desert swallows him whole.

2 — Stranded

A sandstorm erupts without warning, tearing through Casey’s equipment and burying his transport skimmer. With no way back and no signal strong enough to reach HQ, Casey shelters inside a half‑collapsed dune cave.

As the storm clears, he notices something metallic beneath the sand — a hatch.

3 — The Hidden Station

Casey uncovers a buried entrance marked with the insignia of the Vexx Empire. The hatch leads into a long‑abandoned research station, preserved beneath the desert for decades.

Inside, he finds containment pods, corrupted energy samples, and logs referencing a forbidden experiment: Replication of Emperor Void’s corruption.

The Vexx Empire had been trying to recreate the same cosmic infection that once nearly destroyed the galaxy.

4 — The Discovery

Casey digs deeper into the station’s archives. He finds comms recordings, research notes, and a partially active terminal describing “Phase 7: Controlled Void Mutation.”

Horrified, he activates his emergency beacon and transmits everything he can to Seron before the terminal overloads.

“Seron… you need to see this. The Vexx were trying to bring him back.”

5 — Extraction

Minutes later, a dark rift tears open in the sand. Seron Darkness steps through, accompanied by two Guardian Operators:

Operator Helia Strix — Aether Recon Specialist, expert in scanning corrupted environments. Operator Darius Holt — Heavy Weapons & Extraction, responsible for securing dangerous artifacts.

Together, they descend into the station, retrieve the research data, and secure the corrupted samples before the structure collapses.

As they leave, Seron looks back at the sinking ruins. “If the Vexx were this close… someone else will try again.”

Casey nods. The Dunes of Time had revealed a secret the galaxy wasn’t ready for.
