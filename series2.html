The Zavila Galaxy Series 2

Series 2 marks the beginning of the Guardians Rising Era, focusing on the fragile peace following the fall of the Spike Empire. The galaxy is rebuilding, but the scars of war remain, and ancient forces begin to awaken.

Synopsis

Guardia and Zavara stand at a crossroads. Reconstruction brings hope, but the outskirts still burn with “Black Smoke,” and the ruins of the Vexx Empire leak toxic remnants of the past. The Guardians must stabilize the galaxy, redeem former enemies, and uncover the secrets of the Vexx Legacy and the Infernal Dimensions.

Stories of the Rising Era

1. The Zavaran Crisis — The hunt for the shapeshifter Arsenio and the awakening of Ral’s Crimson Form.

2. The Embers of Reform — Miles Church works to heal the corrupted outskirts and discovers Lilia Spike.

3. The Vexx Legacy — A high‑risk infiltration mission to recover elite Vexx blueprints from a crashed Dreadnought.

4. The Void Descendant — Lilia Spike trains to master the Oblivion‑Strain and saves the Nexus‑Hub.

5. Shadows of the Starline — Tom Letanio travels to the Red Lake of Zavara to reclaim the lost Starline archives.

6. The Hell‑Gate Catalyst — HawkBat Rogue descends into Ignis Vortem to secure the power source for Project: Nexus.

Expanded Galactic Locations

Planet Guardia — Heart of Guardian operations.

Planet Zavara — Violet‑hued world with dangerous Under‑Tunnels and the Red Lake.

The Nexus‑Hub — Central station for trade and refuge.

Ignis Vortem — Volcanic world tied to Project Nexus.

The Vexx Crash Site — Iron Graveyard of the Dreadnought.

The Old Burning Forest — Reality‑warped ruins on Guardia.

The Void Pocket — A dimension of shifting entropy.
