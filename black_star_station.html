Black Star Station

Black Star Station is the Guardians’ first fully operational space‑station headquarters, constructed in 2036 and named in honor of Seron Darkness’s Dark Star Essence. Inspired by Angelo Pierce’s legendary Starline engineering, Seron envisioned a station that could serve as a home, a fortress, and a command center for the entire Zavila Galaxy.

1 — The Idea from Angelo

During a mission with Angelo Pierce, Seron witnessed the design of the Starline Marauder — a ship built not just for battle, but for living. Angelo’s philosophy was simple:

“A warrior fights better when he has a place worth returning to.”

Seron carried that idea with him. Years later, after the rise of the Guardians, he proposed the creation of a massive orbital HQ — a place where Guardians could train, rest, and operate without fear of planetary attack.

2 — Construction of the Station

Built in Zavara’s upper orbit, Black Star Station spans several kilometers and contains:

• Guardian living quarters

• A full cafeteria

• A gaming & recreation hall

• Aether training chambers

• A command bridge

• A Dark Star reactor core (stabilized by Seron)

The station is so large that it functions like a floating city — a home for Guardians across the galaxy.

3 — Moving In

The Guardians spent weeks setting up their rooms, customizing their quarters, and exploring the endless halls of the station. Cody Kritz immediately claimed the gaming room. Tom Letanio set up a meditation chamber. Angelis “Shifter” Darkness claimed the darkest corner of the station — naturally.

Seron, meanwhile, oversaw the installation of the Dark Star Core — a stabilized fragment of his cosmic essence that powers the entire station.

4 — A New Era for the Guardians

With Black Star Station operational, the Guardians finally had a headquarters worthy of their mission. It became:

• A sanctuary for Guardians recovering from missions.

• A fortress against cosmic threats.

• A symbol of unity across the Zavila Galaxy.

Seron stood on the command bridge, looking out at Zavara below. “This is our home now,” he said. “A home built from darkness — to protect the light.”

5 — Legacy

Black Star Station would go on to become the heart of the Guardians — the birthplace of countless missions, victories, and legends. And though few know it, its existence began with a simple idea from a space pirate captain who believed in giving warriors a place to belong.
