Seron’s Life After Beyond The Rift

Five months after the Beyond The Rift incident, Seron Darkness entered the darkest period of his life. David Spike’s fall into corruption shattered him, reshaping him into a hardened, merciless hero who patrolled Guardia City with lethal precision. This era is known as the Dark Star Vigilante Period.

1 — The Fall That Broke Him

David Spike’s corruption was the moment that changed Seron forever. He blamed himself — for being too slow, too weak, too trusting. For five months, Seron refused to rest, sleep, or return to normal Guardian operations.

He hunted criminals across Guardia City with full force, no restraint, no hesitation.

“If I couldn’t save David… I’ll save everyone else by force.”

2 — The Criminal Underworld of Guardia City

Seron descended into the deepest layers of the city — the tunnels, the abandoned factories, the black‑market districts. Criminals whispered his name like a curse.

He fought gangs, syndicates, smugglers, and Aether‑dealers. Many surrendered at the sight of him. Others weren’t so lucky.

Seron became a myth — a shadow that struck without warning.

3 — The Green Bandana

One night, Seron infiltrated the Criminal Underworld Market, a hidden bazaar beneath Guardia City. Among the stolen weapons and illegal Aether, he found a simple cloth:

A green bandana with a shark‑teeth pattern.

He tied it around his neck. It became his symbol — a reminder of the predator he had become.

The bandana would later become one of Seron’s most iconic pieces of clothing.

4 — The Red Star Graffiti

Weeks later, Seron traveled through the Kacia Desert hunting a smuggling ring. On a ruined stone wall, he found a symbol painted in red:

A Red Star.

Something about it resonated with him — a symbol of rebellion, survival, and identity.

This graffiti became the inspiration for the Red Star emblem he would later wear on his shirt.

Seron wore normal clothing during this era — jeans, boots, a jacket, and the green bandana. The Red Star symbol completed the look that would define him for years.

5 — Building the Guardians HQ

Two months after the Red Star discovery, Seron finally slowed down. He returned to the forest where he, Connor Violet, and David Spike had their first camping hangout as young adults.

There, he made a decision:

To build something new. Something better. Something that would prevent another David Spike incident.

On that forest ground, Seron constructed the first version of the Guardians Headquarters.

It was small at first — a bunker, a training yard, a comms tower. But it grew into the legendary HQ known across the Zavila Galaxy.

6 — The End of the Dark Star Vigilante Period

Building the HQ gave Seron purpose again. It pulled him out of the darkness — not fully, but enough to stand again.

The Dark Star Vigilante Period ended, but its scars shaped the leader he would become.

Seron Darkness emerged stronger, colder, and more determined than ever.
