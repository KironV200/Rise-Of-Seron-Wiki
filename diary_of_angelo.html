The Diary Of Angelo

The Diary Of Angelo chronicles the early life of Angelo Pierce — the Universal Starline Captain, the feared Space Pirate Commander, and the man who would one day become the Emperor of Zavara. These entries take place in 2002, long before Angelo met Seron Darkness or the Guardians, during the era when he carved his legend across the Zavila Galaxy.

1 — Entry I: The Starline Captain

Angelo Pierce commands the Starline Marauder, a pirate vessel feared across the outer systems. His crew follows him not out of fear — but out of respect. Angelo is a strategist, a fighter, and a man who refuses to bow to any empire.

His diary begins with a simple line: “The galaxy is too big to follow someone else’s rules.”

2 — Entry II: Arrival on Zavara

Angelo first lands on Zavara after a hyperspace malfunction forces him to crash‑land in the outskirts of the planet’s crystalline forests. Expecting hostility, he finds instead a world untouched by war — a world with potential.

He writes: “Zavara is quiet. Too quiet. A place like this shouldn’t exist… but it does.”

Angelo begins exploring the planet, mapping its terrain, studying its wildlife, and learning its secrets. Slowly, Zavara becomes more than a stop — it becomes a home.

3 — Entry III: The Battle Journal

Angelo’s diary shifts into a battle log as he encounters raiders, mercenaries, and remnants of the Vexx Empire attempting to claim Zavara for themselves. Angelo fights them off with brutal precision, recording each encounter in detail.

“If Zavara is to be mine, I must defend it.”

These entries show the birth of the warrior Zavara would one day call its protector.

4 — Entry IV: The Hidden Caverns

Angelo discovers a network of ancient caverns beneath Zavara’s surface. Inside, he finds relics, star maps, and ruins belonging to civilizations older than the Vexx Empire.

He writes: “This planet is not just a home. It is a vault of forgotten power.”

These discoveries shape Angelo’s destiny — and Zavara’s future.

5 — Entry V: Becoming Zavara’s Guardian

Angelo’s final entries describe his decision to stay on Zavara permanently. He fortifies the planet, builds outposts, and establishes the first Starline Watch — a precursor to the Guardians he would one day meet.

“If the galaxy wants Zavara… they’ll have to go through me.”

This marks the end of the diary — and the beginning of Angelo’s rise.
