Welcome to the Stories category. Here you can explore every saga, arc, and series in the Rise Of Seron universe.

Current Sagas / Storylines

These are the active, ongoing narrative arcs currently shaping the Rise Of Seron universe. Each saga contains multiple interconnected stories that build the larger canon.

Saga 1: The Zavara Heist Saga

The first major saga following the discovery of Prism Order activity and the traitor within the Guardians.

The Zavara Heist

Seron, Tom, and Cody uncover Arsenio’s hidden operations and the Prism Order’s return.

Dunes Of Time

Casey Vigor becomes stranded in the Kacia Desert and discovers a buried Vexx research station.

The Prism Order

Seron, Tom, and Cody explore the Prism Order’s lab and uncover the rogue Guardian conspiracy.

Reach For The Stars

Tom Letanio discovers the truth — Vlasta Jeronim is the traitor working with the Prism Order.

Under Your Knife

The finale — Tom hunts down Vlasta in the Aether Mines and ends the Prism Order for good.

Saga 2: The Blood Moon Saga

The second saga, beginning immediately after the Zavara Heist, exploring the mysteries of OverDarkis and the Blood Moon phenomenon.

The Blood Moon Party

The Guardians attend Andrew Overdark’s Blood Moon Festival — until Bloodborn Husks rise.

The Blood Moon’s Light

Marcel Toxin investigates the Blood Moon and discovers the symbol of the Fogborn Syndicate.

Series 1 — The Zavila Galaxy Series 1

The origin season of the Zavila Galaxy. Features 11 major stories, character awakenings, cosmic threats, and the first full-scale war of Guardia.

Series 2 — The Guardians Rising Era

The second major season of the Zavila Galaxy saga. Follows the aftermath of the Spike Empire’s fall, the rise of new threats, and the Guardians’ transition into galactic peacekeepers.

The Zavila Galaxy Series 3

The Hell Era — a multi‑dimensional war spanning Pandemonium, Zavara, and Guardians HQ. Featuring Tom Letanio’s Zenith Form, the rise of Lord Movkos, and the sealing of the Infernal Dimensions.

The Civil War — Chapter 1: The War Unnoticed

Casey Vigor’s powers malfunction, Nova Watch approaches him with lies, and Seron declares a Civil War after a confrontation at the Vexx Empire Crash Site.

The Zavara Heist

Seron Darkness, Tom Letanio, and Cody Kritz hunt Arsenio Beau Innes through abandoned Zavaran mines, uncovering the truth behind his past and his planned heist on the Zavaran Power Plant.

The Zavila Galaxy Series 4

The Chronos & Ignis Era — a dual‑saga exploring temporal collapse, ancient flame entities, and the rise of Babylon beneath Ignis Vortem.

Casey Vigor: The Dunes Of Time

Casey Vigor becomes stranded in the Kacia Desert and uncovers a buried Vexx Empire research station attempting to replicate Emperor Void’s corruption.

The Prism Order

The Zavara Heist Part 2 — Seron, Tom, and Cody uncover a Prism Order lab experimenting on civilians, revealing a conspiracy involving the Vexx Empire and rogue Guardian members.

The Diary Of Angelo

The early journals of Angelo Pierce — the Universal Starline Captain — chronicling his battles, discoveries, and the story of how Zavara became his home long before he met Seron or the Guardians.

Black Star Station

In 2036, Seron creates the Guardians’ first space‑station HQ — inspired by Angelo Pierce — a massive orbital fortress powered by Dark Star Essence.

Reach For The Stars

Tom Letanio uncovers a Prism Order lab in the Kacia Desert and discovers the truth — Vlasta Jeronim is the traitor within the Guardians.

The Blood Moon Party

Tom, Seron, and Kole attend the first Blood Moon Festival on OverDarkis — until Bloodborn Husks attack, powered by a Blood Moon Crystal that Tom destroys.

The Blood Moon's Light

Marcel Toxin investigates the Blood Moon on OverDarkis and discovers a second Blood Moon Crystal — and the ancient symbol of the Fogborn Syndicate.

Under Your Knife

The finale of the Zavara Heist storyline — Tom hunts down Vlasta Jeronim in the Aether Mines and ends the Prism Order once and for all.
