The Blood Moon Party

The Blood Moon Party is the first annual celebration held on OverDarkis, the homeworld of Black Rose (Andrew Overdark). Five months after the Zavara Heist and the Prism Order revelations, the Guardians finally attend a peaceful event — or so they thought.

1 — Arrival on OverDarkis

Tom Letanio, Seron Darkness, and Kole Rogue arrive on OverDarkis for the first time. The planet is covered in neon forests, obsidian mountains, and crimson skies — a perfect setting for the Blood Moon Festival.

Black Rose greets them personally, excited to show his homeworld’s traditions.

Black Rose: “Tonight, the Blood Moon rises. And trust me — it’s beautiful.”

2 — The Blood Moon Rises

As the party begins, music echoes through the crystal canyons. Drinks, dancing, and celebration fill the night.

Then the sky shifts. A massive red moon rises — glowing brighter than any star.

The crowd cheers. The Guardians watch in awe.

3 — The Humanoid Zombies

Suddenly, the ground cracks. Red mist pours from the soil.

Humanoid zombies — known locally as Bloodborn Husks — rise from the earth.

They are not infectious. They are not undead. They are energy constructs created by the Blood Moon’s power.

The party erupts into chaos as the Husks swarm the festival grounds.

4 — The Guardians Fight Back

Seron unleashes Dark Star blasts, tearing through waves of Husks. Kole Rogue uses his elemental Aether to shield civilians. Tom Letanio charges forward, fists glowing with Phoenix Aether.

Tom: “These things aren’t alive — something’s powering them!”

5 — The Blood Moon Crystal

Tom spots a glowing red shard embedded in a stone altar — the Blood Moon Crystal.

It pulses with the same energy as the Husks.

Tom leaps onto the altar, channels his full strength, and smashes the crystal with a single Phoenix‑charged punch.

The crystal shatters. The Husks collapse instantly.

6 — Aftermath

The party slowly resumes as the Blood Moon fades. Black Rose apologizes — but the Guardians laugh it off.

Kole: “Honestly? Best party I’ve ever been to.”

Seron: “Next time, maybe warn us about the zombies.”

Tom keeps a shard of the shattered crystal — a reminder of the night the Blood Moon tried to crash the party.
