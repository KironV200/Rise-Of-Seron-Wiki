Lore

Explore the history, eras, and hidden stories of the Rise Of Seron universe.

The Golden Trio (1992–1999)

The early years of Seron Darkness, Connor Violet, and David Spike — before the Rift changed everything.

Beyond The Rift

The tragic mission where David Spike was taken by Emperor Void, corrupted with false memories, and Seron Darkness was secretly infected by the Void — marking the beginning of his cold, determined transformation.

The Angelic Purges

A dark era where the Celestial Host invaded Guardia and Zavara, attempting to exterminate Aetherians and anyone born with super‑powers. This event shaped the early lives of Seron, Connor, and David, and forged the Golden Trio through shared trauma and resistance.

The Battle of Guardia

The largest conflict in modern history — the catastrophic war between Seron Darkness and a Void‑corrupted David Spike. This battle nearly destroyed Guardia City and marked the final collapse of the Golden Trio.

Aetherians

Aether‑powered humans in the ROS universe — born or created — with types such as Temporal, Dark Energy, Elemental, Adaptive, and Corrupted Aether. and more.

Seron After Beyond The Rift

The 5‑month vigilante era where Seron became a hardened dark hero, gained his iconic symbols, and built the first Guardians HQ.

King Indigo

The story of how Tom Letanio became the savior of Hell and was crowned King Indigo by Lucifer Morningstar.
