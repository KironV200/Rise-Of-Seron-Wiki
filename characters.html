Characters

Seron “Super” Darkness

Seron is the omniversal god, the creator of the ROS Universe and The Zavila Galaxy. His power is infinite, his darkness controlled and intelligent, and his role as the main hero defines the fate of all existence.

Kole Rogue

Kole is one of the earliest warriors shaped by Seron’s influence. His abilities, resilience, and connection to the Zavila Galaxy make him a key figure in the early eras of the omniversal saga.

Angelis “Shifter” Darkness

Angelis is a high‑tier Guardian known for his dimensional shifting abilities. Born with an unstable connection to void‑energy, he learned to control it through Seron’s guidance. Angelis can phase through matter, warp short distances, and temporarily split into parallel echoes of himself. His combat style is fast, unpredictable, and lethal to corrupted entities.

Despite his power, Angelis carries a heavy burden — every shift risks tearing open micro‑fractures in reality. His discipline and loyalty to Seron make him one of the most trusted Guardians in the Zavila Galaxy.

Angelo Pierce

Angelo is a legendary space‑faring tactician and former commander of the Universal Starline. His flagship vanished during a catastrophic battle, only to be rediscovered decades later — intact when it should have been destroyed. Angelo’s knowledge of cosmic warfare, starship systems, and contingency planning makes him an invaluable ally to Seron.

He is known for creating contingency plans for every Guardian, not out of distrust, but out of respect for the destructive potential they carry. Angelo’s return marks the beginning of a new era in the ROS Universe.

Mason “Killer” Darkness

Mason is a Xanian‑born Guardian whose raw physical power rivals early cosmic titans. Nicknamed “Killer” during the Fracture Era, Mason earned his reputation by single‑handedly eliminating void‑spawned monstrosities that threatened the Zavila Galaxy.

Though feared by enemies, Mason is fiercely loyal to Seron and the Guardians. His Xanian biology grants him enhanced regeneration, extreme durability, and the ability to channel destructive kinetic bursts. Mason is a frontline warrior who thrives in impossible battles.

Michael Rogue

Michael Rogue is a strategic Guardian whose intuition and adaptability make him one of the most reliable operatives in the Zavila Galaxy. Known for his sharp perception and ability to read cosmic anomalies, Michael often serves as the bridge between Seron’s will and the Guardians’ missions.

His connection to the Rogue lineage grants him heightened awareness, rapid learning capability, and a unique resistance to void corruption. Michael’s presence stabilizes teams, and his leadership has prevented multiple large‑scale catastrophes across the ROS Universe.

Marcel Toxin

Marcel is a biochemical prodigy whose body produces adaptive toxin‑energy — a rare mutation originating from deep within the Zavila Galaxy. His toxins evolve in real time, allowing him to counter enemy abilities, dissolve void‑corruption, or reinforce allies with biochemical enhancements.

Though feared by many, Marcel is one of Seron’s most valuable specialists, capable of neutralizing threats that even Guardians struggle to confront.

Casey Vigor

Casey is a high‑energy combatant whose strength scales with emotional intensity. His Vigor Core — a rare internal energy source — allows him to unleash explosive bursts of power, accelerate his movements, and withstand devastating attacks.

Casey’s loyalty to Seron is unwavering, and his presence on the battlefield often turns the tide of entire conflicts across the Zavila Galaxy.

Connor Violet

Connor is a tactical Guardian known for his sharp instincts and violet‑spectrum energy manipulation. His energy constructs, shields, and precision blasts make him one of the most versatile fighters in the ROS Universe.

Despite his youth, Connor’s discipline and strategic mind have earned him Seron’s respect — and a reputation as one of the most reliable Guardians in active duty.

David Spike

David is a hardened warrior forged through relentless conflict. His signature ability, Spike‑Force, allows him to generate razor‑sharp kinetic spikes from his body, turning him into a living weapon capable of piercing even void‑born armor.

David’s aggressive combat style is balanced by his fierce loyalty to his allies, making him a frontline powerhouse in Guardian operations.

Ral Awakening

Ral is a cold, execution‑focused Guardian shaped by Seron’s darker teachings. His Awakening State allows him to channel crimson energy that amplifies his strength, speed, and killing precision to terrifying levels.

Ral rarely speaks, but when he acts, entire battlefields shift. His presence alone is enough to break enemy morale.

Riven Solari

Riven is a solar‑born warrior who channels radiant star‑energy. His Solari Core grants him the ability to generate blinding light, solar blades, and high‑temperature energy waves capable of cutting through void corruption.

As one of the brightest forces in the ROS Universe, Riven serves as a counterbalance to the darker energies wielded by other Guardians — proving that Seron’s order thrives on balance.
