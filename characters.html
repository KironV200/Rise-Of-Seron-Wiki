Characters

Seron “Super” Darkness

Seron is the primordial architect of the universe — a former human who ascended after surviving the collapse of his original reality. Absorbed into a dying Dark Star, he awakened as a cosmic entity capable of shaping galaxies, dimensions, and the laws of existence itself.

Calm, distant, and impossibly powerful, Seron serves as the silent stabilizing force behind the Zavila Galaxy. His influence is woven into every star and every event, even when he does not appear directly.

Kole Rogue

Kole Rogue — known throughout the Zavila Galaxy as The Arsonist — is one of the earliest and most unstable Seron-Type clones ever created. Born from an experimental attempt to replicate Seron’s cosmic structure, Kole inherited only fragments of Seron’s power, but those fragments manifested as uncontrollable thermal energy and violent emotional surges.

Kole fights with a molten fire axe capable of melting steel and igniting entire battlefields. His body constantly radiates heat, and his presence alone can warp the air around him. Though aggressive, reckless, and driven by instinct, Kole is not inherently evil — he is a fractured being struggling to understand his identity and purpose.

His unpredictable nature makes him a dangerous wildcard in the galaxy. Sometimes an enemy, sometimes an ally, and sometimes a force of nature acting on pure emotion, Kole’s actions often shift the balance of major conflicts. His full story, abilities, and origins are detailed on his dedicated page.

Angelis “Shifter” Darkness

Angelis is a Seron‑type clone who rejected cosmic destiny and instead became the galaxy’s most feared street‑level hunter. Born without Seron’s cosmic abilities, Angelis developed heightened reflexes, brutal efficiency, and a predatory instinct that earned him the title The Night Hunter.

Operating in the darkest corners of the Zavila Galaxy, Angelis hunts criminals, corrupt officials, and violent offenders using a SPAS‑12 shotgun, a Desert Eagle, dual pistols, and his signature crowbar. His combat style is ruthless, fast, and grounded — the opposite of Seron’s cosmic grace.

Cold, intense, and morally uncompromising, Angelis is the shadow Seron never wanted to become — but sometimes needed. He is the clone who fights the battles no one else sees, protecting the innocent through fear, precision, and relentless pursuit.

Angelo Pierce

Angelo is a legendary space‑faring tactician and former commander of the Universal Starline. His flagship vanished during a catastrophic battle, only to be rediscovered decades later — intact when it should have been destroyed. Angelo’s knowledge of cosmic warfare, starship systems, and contingency planning makes him an invaluable ally to Seron.

He is known for creating contingency plans for every Guardian, not out of distrust, but out of respect for the destructive potential they carry. Angelo’s return marks the beginning of a new era in the ROS Universe.

Mason “Killer” Darkness

Mason is a Xanian‑born Guardian whose raw physical power rivals early cosmic titans. Nicknamed “Killer” during the Fracture Era, Mason earned his reputation by single‑handedly eliminating void‑spawned monstrosities that threatened the Zavila Galaxy.

Though feared by enemies, Mason is fiercely loyal to Seron and the Guardians. His Xanian biology grants him enhanced regeneration, extreme durability, and the ability to channel destructive kinetic bursts. Mason is a frontline warrior who thrives in impossible battles.

Michael Rogue

Michael Rogue is the most emotionally stable of the Seron‑type clones — a rare creation whose identity formed around compassion, restraint, and clarity rather than chaos. While he lacks Seron’s cosmic scale, he possesses something equally valuable: a balanced mind capable of wielding temporal power without corruption.

Michael carries two signature tools: a stolen temporal pocket watch once owned by the tyrant Grayson Talon, and a guitar‑axe that channels harmonic energy into shockwaves and stabilizing fields. These abilities make him one of the most versatile Guardians in the Zavila Galaxy.

Calm, empathetic, and morally grounded, Michael often acts as the emotional anchor among the clones. He is the one who chooses understanding over domination, and time over chaos — a stabilizing force in a universe shaped by conflict.

Riven Solari

Riven Solari is a half‑angel warrior who defected from the celestial regime after uncovering the truth behind their purges. His hybrid nature grants him immense physical power, but his true weapon is Emperor Red, a temporal Stand capable of erasing or skipping segments of time.

Calm, disciplined, and morally grounded, Riven fights not for revenge but for justice. His knowledge of angelic warfare and his temporal abilities make him one of the most unpredictable allies in the Zavila Galaxy.

David Spike

Once Seron’s closest friend, David Spike was consumed by a collapsing star‑void that rewrote his memories and twisted his body into a vessel of entropy. Now known as The Black Nebula, David wields destructive entropy energy capable of decaying matter, destabilizing regions, and tearing through cosmic barriers.

His corruption is fueled by a lie — the belief that Seron abandoned him. This false memory drives his rage, making him one of the most tragic and catastrophic threats in the ROS Universe.

Connor Violet

Connor Violet, known as Captain Violet, is a Serlium‑enhanced warrior who fights with a blue cyber revolver and the legendary Serlium Shield — a near‑indestructible artifact capable of absorbing cosmic, elemental, and temporal attacks.

Tactical, loyal, and unwaveringly brave, Connor stands as one of Seron’s most trusted allies. His presence on the battlefield is a symbol of stability and hope across the Zavila Galaxy.

Ral Awakening

Ral Awakening is a fear‑driven executioner whose trauma‑born awakening granted him terrifying abilities. Wielding Crimson Fear — a blade that amplifies terror — and reinforced chains, Ral fights with a brutal, unpredictable hybrid style.

Cold, unstable, and sadistic, Ral spreads psychological devastation wherever he appears. He is one of the most dangerous independent threats in the ROS Universe.

Casey Vigor

Casey Vigor is the last survivor of the Vigor bloodline — a family once known for their rare isotope‑based abilities. After witnessing the extermination of his lineage, Casey underwent Isotope Ascension, transforming into an immortal nuclear entity.

Calm, patient, and quietly powerful, Casey channels his trauma into purpose. His nuclear abilities make him a stabilizing force in cosmic conflicts and a symbol of survival across the Zavila Galaxy.

Marcel Toxin

Marcel Toxin is a biochemical genius whose own experiments reshaped his body. A catastrophic lab accident altered his lungs and nerves, forcing him to breathe toxin‑filtered air and leaving him unable to feel physical touch.

Wielding a green bio‑energy scythe and a mind sharpened by scientific obsession, Marcel treats the battlefield like a laboratory. Detached but not heartless, he walks the line between hero and hazard.

Nathan Ice

Nathan Ice is the calm and disciplined King of the Iceberg Tower — a frozen fortress in the far north of the Zavila Galaxy. As an Ice Elemental Ascendant, Nathan commands glacial energy, sub‑zero temperatures, and crystalline constructs with unmatched precision.

Forced into leadership after the Angelic Purges claimed his parents, Nathan grew into a protective and strategic monarch. His power is fueled not by rage, but by duty — making him one of the galaxy’s most stable and respected elemental rulers.

Samuel “Wave” Andreas

Wave is Guardia City’s beloved Comedian Hero — a fast‑talking, joke‑cracking fighter whose humor is as iconic as his abilities. Born with a rare adaptive mimic mutation, Wave can instantly copy the powers of anyone he sees, making him one of the most unpredictable heroes in the galaxy.

His jokes aren’t just for show — they keep allies calm, throw enemies off rhythm, and turn fear into confidence. Beneath the humor is a compassionate hero who refuses to let darkness win.

Andrew OverDark

Andrew OverDark — known as the Black Rose — is one of the most feared temporal beings in existence. Once an ordinary man, Andrew awakened catastrophic time‑breaking power after witnessing the murder of his wife during the Angelic Purges.

Consumed by grief and sleep‑deprived fury, Andrew hunted the angelic host across timelines, erasing their leader Adam from existence. He is not evil — but he is merciless, a god forged from heartbreak whose power reshaped cosmic history.

Tom Letanio

Tom Letanio is a disciplined temporal guardian who wields Indigo Fist — a powerful close‑range Stand capable of stopping time indefinitely. Alongside his Stand, Tom commands the Blue Phoenix, a celestial spirit of rebirth and blue flame.

Traumatized by the loss of his parents during the Angelic Purges, Tom forged himself into a calm, controlled protector. His mastery of time, phoenix fire, and guardian discipline makes him one of the most reliable defenders in the Zavila Galaxy.

Grayson Talon

Grayson Talon, known across the galaxy as The Pocket Watch Tyrant, is a brilliant but unstable temporal engineer whose experiments shattered his mind into multiple elemental personalities. Grayson created the legendary Temporal Pocket Watch, the same artifact later stolen and used by Michael Rogue.

Once a respected chronomancer, Grayson pushed too far into forbidden time‑fracture research. The backlash split his consciousness into alternate personas — each with its own elemental affinity and combat style. Grayson himself remains the cold, calculating core personality, obsessed with reclaiming his lost inventions and restoring his fractured identity.

Ignis Blaze

Ignis Blaze is the volatile fire‑elemental personality born from Grayson Talon’s fractured mind. Unlike Grayson’s cold precision, Ignis is pure instinct — a living inferno driven by emotion, destruction, and raw heat.

Ignis manifests whenever Grayson’s rage overwhelms his control. In this form, he wields Pyro‑Flux, a burning aura capable of melting steel, igniting air, and overwhelming opponents with explosive flame bursts. Ignis is unpredictable, aggressive, and nearly impossible to reason with — the embodiment of Grayson’s suppressed fury.

Finn Sealot

Finn Sealot is the Solaris‑aligned personality created when Grayson Talon’s mind fractured under temporal overload. Calm, radiant, and eerily serene, Finn represents Grayson’s idealized self — the version of him untouched by corruption or obsession.

Finn commands Solaris Energy, channeling concentrated sunlight into beams, shields, and purifying blasts capable of cleansing corrupted matter. Unlike Ignis, Finn is peaceful and philosophical, often appearing to guide or stabilize Grayson’s collapsing psyche. But when threatened, his Solaris power becomes one of the most destructive forces in the galaxy.

Esteban Maverick

Known across the coastlines as The Gunslinger of Beach City, Esteban Maverick is a fast‑talking, sharp‑shooting Guardian whose confidence borders on arrogance — and he earns every bit of it. His dual pistols, Sunrise and High Tide, channel kinetic Aether rounds capable of piercing armor and destabilizing corrupted entities.

Esteban joined Team Crusader after saving an entire district during the Angelic Purges, and Seron personally recruited him into the Guardians for his precision, adaptability, and fearless attitude. Beneath the cocky exterior, Esteban is fiercely loyal and hides a strategic mind sharper than his aim.

Royal Demetrios

Royal Demetrios is the 2nd Time Breaker God — a being capable of bending temporal flow, rewriting micro‑timelines, and freezing causality itself. As the Leader of Team Crusader, Royal carries the weight of countless timelines on his shoulders, leaving him serious, calculating, and perpetually tired.

Despite his exhaustion, Royal remains one of the most reliable Guardians in existence. His mastery of Chrono‑Aether allows him to predict enemy movements, counter multiversal threats, and stabilize fractured dimensions. Royal rarely smiles, but when he does, it’s usually because someone finally impressed him.

Peter Hydros

Peter Hydros — widely known as Electro Boy — is a Guardian whose pure electric abilities make him the livewire of Team Crusader. His personality is a chaotic blend of humor, goofy confidence, and sudden poetic wisdom that appears at the most unexpected moments.

Peter generates and manipulates raw electricity, allowing him to unleash lightning bursts, charge his body with voltage, accelerate his movements, and overload enemy systems. Despite his comedic nature, Peter becomes sharply serious when the situation demands it — especially when protecting Royal and Esteban, whom he considers his closest brothers.
