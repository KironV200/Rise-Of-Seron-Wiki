Reach For The Stars

Reach For The Stars follows Tom Letanio as he is deployed to the Kacia Desert to recover stolen Aether Crystal Crates — crates that should only exist deep within Zavara’s mines. What begins as a simple retrieval mission becomes a revelation that shakes the Guardians to their core.

1 — The Mission Begins

Seron dispatches Tom to the Kacia Desert after sensors detect Zavaran‑origin Aether Crates scattered across the dunes. Tom arrives under blistering heat, his Phoenix Aether shielding him from the worst of the storm winds.

Three crates lie half‑buried in the sand — cracked open, leaking unstable Aether.

“These were stolen… but by who?” Tom mutters.

2 — The Hidden Lab

A faint vibration beneath the sand leads Tom to a concealed hatch. He forces it open and descends into a dimly lit underground facility marked with a symbol he instantly recognizes: The Prism Order.

Inside, he finds:

• Aether mutation chambers

• Crates identical to Zavara’s mine shipments

• Logs detailing forced Aether infusion experiments

The Prism Order had been experimenting on civilians again — this time using Zavaran Aether.

3 — Contacting Seron

Tom activates his comms and sends a direct link to Seron.

Tom: “Seron… you need to see this. The Prism Order is back.”

Seron: “Send everything. I’m on my way.”

As Tom continues searching the lab, he finds encrypted logs — logs that mention a Guardian operative by name.

4 — The Traitor Revealed

Tom decrypts the final log. His heart sinks.

“Supply transfers approved by: Vlasta Jeronim.”

Vlasta — one of their own. A Guardian Operator. A trusted ally.

Tom clenches his fists. “She’s been feeding them intel… and Aether.”

He immediately contacts Seron again.

Tom: “Seron… we have a traitor. It’s Vlasta Jeronim.”

Seron: “…Understood. Stay where you are. I’m coming.”

5 — The Beginning of the Hunt

As Tom waits for Seron’s arrival, he looks around the ruined lab — the experiments, the stolen Aether, the betrayal.

Vlasta Jeronim had been working with the Prism Order from the beginning.

And now the Guardians would have to face the truth: one of their own had turned against them.

The hunt for Vlasta had begun.
