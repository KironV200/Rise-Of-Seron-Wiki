The Angelic Purges

The Angelic Purges were one of the darkest eras in the history of the Zavila Galaxy — a seven‑year campaign where the Celestial Host descended upon Planet Guardia and Planet Zavara with a single mission:

“Cleanse the galaxy of Aetherians and anyone born with super‑powers.”

The Angels believed that mortals with awakened abilities were “unstable anomalies” capable of fracturing the cosmic balance. Their solution was absolute extermination.

Origins of the Purge

The Purges began when the Celestial Council detected a surge of Aetherian births across the galaxy. Fearing a repeat of the ancient Aetherian Uprising, the Angels launched a pre‑emptive strike — without warning, without negotiation, without mercy.

Guardia City was the first to fall under attack. Zavara followed days later.

Targets of the Angels

The Angels hunted:

  • Aetherians
  • Elemental‑born children
  • Super‑powered civilians
  • Guardians‑in‑training
  • Anyone who sheltered powered individuals

Entire districts were burned. Families were torn apart. The Angels called it “purification.” The galaxy called it genocide.

Seron, Connor, and David During the Purges

Before they became legends, Seron Darkness, Connor Violet, and David Spike were young heroes caught in the middle of the chaos. They fought side‑by‑side, saving civilians, evacuating districts, and resisting the Angels wherever they appeared.

These battles forged their bond — the foundation of the Golden Trio.

The End of the Purges

The Purges ended only when the Angels were forced to retreat after the rise of several powerful defenders — including Seron himself. But the scars they left behind shaped the galaxy forever.
