The Angelic Purges

The Angelic Purges were a catastrophic era in the Zavila Galaxy — a seven‑year crusade where the Celestial Host descended upon Guardia and Zavara to eliminate Aetherians and anyone born with super‑powers.

Origins of the Purge

The Celestial Council detected a sudden rise in Aetherian births and declared them a threat to cosmic balance. Without warning, the Angels launched a full extermination campaign.

Targets of the Angels

The Angels hunted:

  • Aetherians
  • Elemental‑born children
  • Super‑powered civilians
  • Guardians‑in‑training
  • Anyone who sheltered powered individuals

The Golden Trio During the Purges

Seron Darkness, Connor Violet, and David Spike fought together during the Purges, protecting civilians and resisting the Angels. These battles forged their legendary bond long before the Rift tore them apart.

The End of the Purges

The Purges ended only when rising defenders — including Seron — forced the Angels to retreat. But the scars left behind shaped the galaxy forever.
