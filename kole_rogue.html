Kole Rogue

Kole Rogue — known across the Zavila Galaxy as The Arsonist — is one of the earliest and most unstable Seron‑Type clones ever created. His existence is a volatile fusion of raw thermal energy, fractured identity, and uncontrollable aggression, making him one of the most dangerous beings derived from Seron “Super” Darkness.

Overview

Created as Seron‑Type Clone Unit 07, Kole inherited only fragments of Seron’s cosmic structure — but those fragments manifested as unstable, destructive heat. His body constantly generates extreme thermal output, and his mind is shaped by instinct, fury, and survival drive.

Kole wields a molten fire axe capable of melting structures, igniting matter, and channeling his internal heat into explosive close‑range attacks. He is unpredictable, confrontational, and thrives in chaos.

Personality

Kole is defined by intensity. He is short‑tempered, confrontational, and driven by a constant internal pressure that feels like a fire he can never extinguish. His emotions override logic, leading him to act first and think later.

Despite his aggression, Kole is not inherently evil. His rage is a byproduct of his unstable creation, and beneath it lies a fractured identity struggling to understand its purpose.

Backstory

Kole was created during an early experiment to replicate Seron’s cosmic structure. The process partially succeeded — instead of a balanced clone, it produced a being whose energy output was overwhelmingly thermal and destructive.

He broke containment moments after awakening, igniting the facility and earning the title The Arsonist. Since then, he has wandered the galaxy as a destructive force, reluctant anti‑hero, and living reminder of the dangers of cloning a cosmic creator.

Abilities

Pyrokinetic Core: Generates extreme heat and ignites surroundings.

Fire Axe Mastery: Channels molten energy into devastating melee attacks.

Thermal Burst: Releases explosive heat waves.

Enhanced Physicality: Stronger and faster than normal humans.

Heat Immunity: Immune to fire, plasma, and extreme temperatures.

Unstable Seron Fragment: Rare power spikes triggered by emotional surges.

Role in the Zavila Galaxy

Kole Rogue is a chaotic wildcard. He is not aligned with any major faction, but his presence often signals destruction, conflict, or unexpected intervention. His actions frequently alter the balance of power, making him a recurring figure in major and minor story arcs.
