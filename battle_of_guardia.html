The Battle of Guardia

The Battle of Guardia was the most devastating conflict in modern history — the day David Spike, corrupted by Void energy, launched a full‑scale assault on Guardia City, forcing Seron Darkness into a battle that shook the galaxy.

Prelude to War

Once a hero and member of the Golden Trio, David Spike fell into corruption after exposure to the Void Rift. His mind fractured, his power multiplied, and his purpose twisted into destruction.

The Invasion

David’s forces tore through Guardia. Buildings collapsed, streets split open, and the sky turned black with Void energy. The Guardians fought desperately but were overwhelmed.

Seron vs David Spike

The duel between Seron and David became the centerpiece of the battle — a clash of cosmic power and corrupted Void energy. Their fight tore through districts and warped the battlefield.

Seron fought to save his friend. David fought to destroy everything he once loved.

The Turning Point

When David unleashed his full Void form, Seron ascended into his Dark Star State. The resulting explosion purified the corruption long enough for the Guardians to intervene.

Aftermath

Guardia City was left in ruins. The Guardians were nearly wiped out. The Golden Trio was shattered forever — but the galaxy survived.
